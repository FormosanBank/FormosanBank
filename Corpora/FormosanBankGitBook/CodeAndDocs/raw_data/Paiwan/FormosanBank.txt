FormosanBank serves as a centralized repository for linguistic data across the 16 extant Formosan languages.
FormosanBank 作為一個集中 式的語料庫，涵蓋了 16 種現存 的臺灣南島語。
a FormosanBank mavan a qepuqepuan a ziting sa pinituaziriukan, napapeliaw itua i wanglu a vecik kata lemuing pakatua kemacalisian setaiwan.

The project aims to make these resources easily accessible for researchers, educators, and indigenous community members to facilitate research, education, and language preservation efforts.
該項目旨在讓 語言學家、教育工作者和臺灣 原住民族能夠輕鬆存取這些資 源，促進研究、教育以及語言 保存工作。

Standardized Format
標準化格式
namaljaluay a vinecikan

FormosanBank uses a standardized XML format developed by the Pangloss Collection to ensure consistency across the corpus. This format organizes data hierarchically, representing texts, sentences, words, and morphemes, with each level annotated as needed. The structure allows for optional elements like translations and audio references, making the data suitable for both linguistic analysis and computational processing.
FormosanBank 使用由 Pangloss Collection 開發的標準化 XML 格式，以確保語料庫的一致性。 這種格式以層級方式組織數 據，顯示文本、句子、詞彙和 語素，並視需求對每一層級進 行注釋。此結構允許包括翻譯 和音檔參考等可選元素，使語 料既適用於語言學分析又合適 於計算處理。
aza FormosanBank liningdjelj paka tingnaw itua i Pangloss Collection, avansika naseljenguaq katua namadjulu a semeqetj tua kakaiyan. lja aicu a sengsengan, maljaluay a pacun ta penuljat a vinecikan katua ljemita a vecik, utauta maqati a sicekaw itua kinkiw katua mareka sengsengan itua tingnaw ljaki matjatjauvalit ta kai, ljaki kitulu ta lemuing.

The format includes attributes for essential metadata, such as citations, copyright information, and language codes, ensuring transparency and proper attribution.
此格式包含基本的後設資料屬 性，如:引用、版權訊息和語 言代碼，確保透明度和正確的 歸屬。
aicu a namaljaluay a vinecikan izuanan mareka ziriu italadj, sedjelj a sipakeljang tua namakuda a sicekaw itua zuma a kinaizuanan, kineljangan a ziriu, banggu nia kai, avansika namakeljang tu paqulid a vinecikan.

By adopting a consistent format, FormosanBank facilitates data sharing, analysis, and integration with other linguistic tools.
透過採用一致的格式， FormosanBank 促進了語料共 享、分析以及與其他語言學工 具的整合。
utauta namaljenguaq a paseljavak, kinkiw sa matevelj katua mareka sitjaracaquan a kakaiyan a sengsengan.
