# FormosanBank AI Use Addendum

Draft version: 0.1
Date: 2026-06-18
Status: review draft, not legal advice

This addendum clarifies FormosanBank's existing Terms of Use and contributor expectations. It is intended to make commercial AI mining of FormosanBank corpora expressly outside the public permission grant.

## Baseline Position

FormosanBank materials are provided for noncommercial research, education, cultural work, language revitalization, and other permitted noncommercial uses, subject to corpus-specific source licenses, contributor requirements, and citation requirements.

Commercial AI Use is prohibited without prior written permission from FormosanBank and any applicable rights holders.

## Relationship to Source Licenses

FormosanBank corpora are often derived from previously published sources. Each corpus remains subject to its source license, contributor agreement, copyright status, privacy constraints, community constraints, and citation requirements. If a corpus page, XML root, dataset card, source repository, or source material states stricter terms, those stricter terms control.

No part of this addendum grants rights in third-party materials that FormosanBank does not control.

Where FormosanBank materials are offered under a Creative Commons NonCommercial license, Commercial AI Use is not licensed. Where FormosanBank materials are made available through a download gate, access request, hosted dataset page, repository, API key, or other distribution mechanism that references this addendum, access is conditioned on this addendum.

## Definitions

"Materials" means FormosanBank corpora and corpus-derived materials, including source text, translations, transcriptions, audio, alignments, annotations, metadata, corpus structure, XML, CSV, JSON, Parquet, dataset cards, documentation, examples, code-generated exports, and substantially similar reformatted versions.

"AI System" means any machine learning, statistical, neural, generative, retrieval, classification, transcription, translation, speech, language, multimodal, embedding, vector-search, or automated decision system.

"AI Use" includes pretraining, training, fine-tuning, continued training, alignment, reinforcement learning, evaluation, benchmarking, validation, red-teaming, prompt tuning, adapter training, embedding, vector indexing, retrieval-augmented generation, synthetic-data generation, data augmentation, dataset construction, model distillation, model improvement, and any substantially similar computational use.

"Commercial AI Use" means AI Use by, for, on behalf of, or substantially benefiting a commercial entity, paid product, paid service, investor-backed product, internal business system, commercial research lab, contractor, customer, data broker, model provider, API provider, or other activity primarily intended for commercial advantage or monetary compensation. Commercial AI Use includes internal research or evaluation by a commercial entity, even if the resulting model is not immediately released.

"Noncommercial Research AI Use" means AI Use by an academic, community, nonprofit, or individual researcher where the use is not primarily intended for commercial advantage, is not performed for a commercial entity, is not used to support a paid product or service, and complies with this addendum.

## Permitted Uses

Subject to source restrictions and attribution requirements, FormosanBank permits:

1. Noncommercial linguistic research.
2. Noncommercial educational use.
3. Noncommercial cultural, archival, and language revitalization work.
4. Noncommercial AI research only if all conditions below are satisfied.
5. Redistribution only when the applicable corpus license permits redistribution and all notices, citations, source restrictions, and this addendum are preserved.

## Conditions for Noncommercial AI Research

Noncommercial Research AI Use is permitted only if the user:

1. Gives clear attribution to FormosanBank and all required upstream sources.
2. States in any model card, paper, dataset card, repository, demo, or release that FormosanBank materials were used.
3. Carries forward the same noncommercial restriction to any released model, adapter, embedding index, benchmark, synthetic dataset, or derivative dataset trained on or derived from the Materials.
4. Does not sell, license, monetize, deploy commercially, or provide paid access to any AI System trained on or derived from the Materials.
5. Does not transfer the trained model, adapter, embedding index, derived dataset, or synthetic dataset to a commercial entity for product development, evaluation, or deployment.
6. Preserves all copyright, attribution, license, TDM, no-commercial-AI, and provenance notices.
7. Stops use and deletes local copies if FormosanBank or a relevant rights holder reasonably identifies a rights, privacy, safety, or community-governance issue.

## Prohibited Uses Without Written Permission

The following uses are not licensed without prior written permission:

1. Commercial AI Use.
2. Selling, sublicensing, renting, or commercially redistributing the Materials.
3. Selling, licensing, hosting, monetizing, or commercially deploying an AI System trained on, fine-tuned on, evaluated on, or otherwise derived from the Materials.
4. Using the Materials to improve a commercial model, commercial API, commercial product, commercial search system, commercial speech system, commercial translation system, or commercial data product.
5. Using the Materials to create embeddings, vector databases, retrieval indexes, synthetic data, benchmarks, or evaluation suites for commercial AI systems.
6. Removing, hiding, altering, or failing to pass through FormosanBank license, attribution, source, provenance, TDM, or no-commercial-AI notices.
7. Circumventing access controls, robots.txt, TDM reservations, technical restrictions, rate limits, or download gates.
8. Laundering the Materials through paraphrase, translation, synthetic-data generation, OCR, ASR, normalization, tokenization, deduplication, aggregation, or format conversion to evade these terms.
9. Claiming a model, dataset, or product is unconnected to FormosanBank when the Materials were used directly or indirectly in its creation, training, evaluation, validation, or improvement.

## Aggressive Clause: Unauthorized Commercial AI Use

This section is intended for access flows where users have notice of this addendum, especially gated downloads, account-based downloads, API access, dataset-card acceptance, or other clickwrap-like access flows.

By accessing, downloading, copying, scraping, mining, processing, or using the Materials for Commercial AI Use without prior written permission, you acknowledge and agree that:

1. Your use is outside the scope of any public license granted by FormosanBank.
2. Your use is a breach of this addendum and may also infringe copyright, database rights, contract rights, moral rights, publicity/privacy rights, Indigenous data governance interests, or other rights depending on the Materials and jurisdiction.
3. You irrevocably grant FormosanBank, the relevant contributors, and the relevant source communities a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, fully paid, sublicensable license to use, reproduce, inspect, evaluate, benchmark, adapt, fine-tune, train on, distribute, publicly perform, publicly display, commercialize, and otherwise exploit any AI System, model weights, adapters, checkpoints, embeddings, vector indexes, synthetic datasets, evaluation datasets, outputs, products, services, documentation, and derivative works created through or benefiting from that unauthorized Commercial AI Use, to the maximum extent permitted by law.
4. You waive, to the maximum extent permitted by law, any claim against FormosanBank, contributors, or source communities arising from their exercise of the license described in this section.

If any part of this section is held unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain effective. If a court or regulator finds the automatic license grant unenforceable, this section still confirms that FormosanBank granted no Commercial AI Use license and reserved all rights.

## Rights Reservation for Text and Data Mining

All TDM, machine learning, data mining, computational analysis, and AI training rights are reserved for Commercial AI Use unless expressly granted in a separate written agreement.

FormosanBank may express this reservation through human-readable terms, dataset metadata, XML metadata, robots.txt, TDMRep, HTTP headers, HTML metadata, PDF metadata, RSL, Content Signals, or any later machine-readable rights-reservation standard. Absence of a machine-readable signal on any particular copy does not waive these terms.

## Attribution

Any permitted use must cite FormosanBank and all required upstream sources.

Recommended FormosanBank citation:

Mohamed, W., Le Ferrand, E., Sung, L.-M., Prud'hommeaux, E., & Hartshorne, J. K. (2024). FormosanBank. Electronic Resource.

AI-related papers, model cards, dataset cards, repositories, and demos must also state:

This work uses FormosanBank materials. FormosanBank prohibits Commercial AI Use without prior written permission.

## Commercial Licensing

Commercial AI Use requires a separate written license. Commercial requests must identify the exact materials, proposed AI Use, downstream products, release plans, and requested commercial rights. FormosanBank may deny any commercial request for any reason.

## Termination

Any violation of this addendum immediately terminates permission to use the Materials. Upon termination, the user must stop using the Materials and delete all copies, derivatives, embeddings, indexes, synthetic datasets, models, adapters, checkpoints, and outputs to the extent technically and legally possible.
